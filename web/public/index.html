Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, 4K Video Reaper is completely free and open source. There are no hidden fees or premium features.

Is it legal to download YouTube videos?

Downloading videos for personal use is generally acceptable, but redistributing copyrighted content is illegal. Always respect copyright laws and YouTube's terms of service.

What video quality can I download?

You can download videos in their original quality, up to 4K (if available). We support various formats including MP4, WebM, and audio-only MP3.

What is Aria2 and why is it recommended?

Aria2 is a lightweight multi-protocol download utility that enables multi-threaded downloading. It significantly increases download speed by creating multiple connections to the server.

Why would I need to use a proxy?

Proxies can help if YouTube is restricted in your region or if you want to maintain privacy. They can also help bypass some download throttling.
